FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you might be wondering before we start.

How long does a project take?
Most websites go from first conversation to launch in about two weeks. Brand identity packages take about 5 business days, and motion graphics packs are delivered within 48 hours. Bigger projects can take a little longer — I'll always give you a clear timeline up front.
Do I really get a free demo first?
Yes. Before you pay anything, I'll build a working demo of your site so you can see exactly what you're getting. No deposit, no commitment, no risk. If you love it, we move forward. If not, no hard feelings.
How many revisions are included?
Every project includes two full rounds of revisions, so we can dial in the details until it feels exactly right. Most clients are happy well before we use them both.
Do I own the website and files?
100%. Once the project is paid in full, everything is yours — the website, the design files, the logo, and all source files in every format. No licensing fees, no lock-in.
Can you combine services?
Absolutely — that's the whole point of one studio. Most clients pair a website with brand identity and SEO so everything is cohesive. Bundle Brand + Web and I'll take $100 off the total.
How do payments work?
Simple flat pricing, paid securely online — no hourly billing or surprise invoices. Typically half to get started and half on launch. We'll confirm everything before any work begins.
