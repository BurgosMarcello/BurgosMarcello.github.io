Dad's Wooden Spoon

A home-based cookie business needed more than a website — they needed a brand. I built them one from the ground up.

Web Design E-commerce Logo Design Brand Identity Photography
dads-wooden-spoon.com

The challenge

The business had no web presence and was relying entirely on word-of-mouth. The owner needed a way to take orders online, build trust with new customers, and present a consistent brand identity.

The approach

I started with the brand — defining a warm, artisan aesthetic with a custom color palette, logo, and typography system. Then I built the site around the product photography to let the cookies do the talking.

The result

A complete brand identity and e-commerce ready site — logo, color system, copy, and a store customers can actually trust. Delivered in under two weeks.
