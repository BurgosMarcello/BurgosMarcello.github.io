Glow Aesthetics

glowaesthetics.co
Look like you, only more rested.

The challenge

Med spas live and die on trust. The studio was competing on Instagram alone and needed a site that felt safe, high-end, and clear about pricing — so new clients would book a consult instead of scrolling past.

The approach

I built an editorial brand — soft, quiet-luxury palette, elegant serif type, and a transparent treatment menu. A recurring membership offer and one-tap consult booking turn first-time visitors into repeat clients.

The result

A polished, conversion-focused site with flat-rate pricing, a $99/month membership, and booking built in — the kind of presence that makes a boutique studio look established. Delivered in under two weeks.
