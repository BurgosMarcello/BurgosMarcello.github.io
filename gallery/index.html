The Gallery

A room built for looking.

Founded in 2016, Meridian occupies a daylit space on West 24th Street in the heart of Chelsea. We represent a small program of artists working in painting and sculpture, with a focus on material honesty and quiet ambition.

Exhibitions rotate every eight weeks. Admission is always free — come slowly, and stay as long as you like.
